End of Summer!

Today's my last day as a
Zynga
Software Engineer Intern. What a journey. Getting to make a game out of scratch and win 2nd place in the Intern Innovation project, working on a large codebase and figuring out how mobile games work (How it logs into facebook, how it updates its notificaiton badges on the home screen..)... What a journey. I have learned so much. I can't thank the full time software engineers who helped me through this. I also made great friends. I hope I'll see them in the future.
