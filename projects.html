SUPPLY AND APPLY
CS 160 Android Task/Supply/Job organization app for Plumbers. Demo video
here.
Went through interviews, prototyping, user experience surveys to figure out the perfect Android App for plumbers. Some tasks include starting a job and adding appliances that need to fixed at a certain location, estimating number of parts to be ordered for the job, and keeping track of what tasks are finished and what parts are left. The app goes through the workflow of adding a new job, adding the appliances that need to be fixed, and ultimately get the estimation of parts needed to complete the job.
